## What This Is

Good infrastructure is invisible. Bad infrastructure is all you think about.

This section documents patterns for the layer beneath your code: database management, deployment, tooling, environment decisions. Not frameworks or libraries. The structural choices that outlive every dependency you'll ever add.

Same format as Fundamentals: a principle, a demonstration, and the counterarguments.
